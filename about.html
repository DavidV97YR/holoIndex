About This Site
What is holoIndex?

holoIndex is an unofficial fan-made calendar for tracking hololive talent birthdays, debut anniversaries, and events — with live countdowns, multi-year archives, and full offline support.

This site is not affiliated with Cover Corporation, hololive production, or any related parties. All talent names, images, and associated content belong to their respective rights holders.

Data Sources

All talent and event data is maintained in Google Sheets published as CSV and fetched on page load. Data is cached locally by the service worker for offline use.
